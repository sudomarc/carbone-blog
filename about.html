About Carbone Notes

Carbone Notes is an independent publication dedicated to capturing ideas, insights, and guides from across the technology, cybersecurity, and lifestyle ecosystems. We believe in writing plainly, researching deeply, and publishing completely in the open.

Automated Intelligence

In addition to our curated editorial pieces, this blog is powered by an automated intelligence engine. Inspired by the open-source thread-bot project, our system fetches fresh news, extracts key technical information or lifestyle data, and uses advanced language models to synthesize them into short, punchy articles.

Instead of generative image fillers, we curate relevant images directly from across the web or maintain a high-quality local asset library to keep the reading experience crisp and meaningful.

Our Stack

  • Frontend: Simple, ultra-lightweight semantic HTML and CSS, styled without bloated frameworks.
  • Automation: Python pipelines running via GitHub Actions on scheduling.
  • Sourcing: News and intelligence compiled via public repositories, web feeds, and language model synthesizers.
